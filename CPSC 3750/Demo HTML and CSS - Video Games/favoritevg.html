My Favorite Video Games

First-Person Shooters (FPS)

FPS games focus on immersive gameplay from the first-person perspective. Here are my favorites:

Call of Duty: MW to Black Ops 2

A series of legendary FPS games with intense campaigns and competitive multiplayer modes. I specifically grew up playing the OG Modern Warefare trilogy and then World at War and Black ops 1 and 2.

Halo Series

A sci-fi FPS series with epic campaigns and groundbreaking multiplayer modes. I grew up with xboxs and so some of my best memories are playing the original halo trilogy with my brother.

Doom (2016)

An adrenaline-pumping shooter with fast-paced combat and iconic demons.

Third-Person Shooters

Third-person shooters combine action-packed combat with a cinematic perspective. Here are some of my favorites:

Gears of War

A thrilling shooter with tactical combat and a gripping narrative.

Red Dead Redemption 2

An open-world third-person shooter that captures the spirit of the Wild West. This game has one of the greatest most emotional stories ever told in gaming

Open-World/Action & Adventure

Open-world games allow for exploration and discovery in expansive, immersive environments.

The Elder Scrolls IV: Oblivion & V: Skyrim

Massive RPGs with limitless exploration and countless quests.

Fallout 3 & New Vegas

Post-apocalyptic open-world RPGs filled with rich storytelling and exploration.

Soulsborne Games

Soulsborne games are known for their challenging combat, intricate level design, and deep lore. These games test patience, skill, and perseverance.

Dark Souls Series

Renowned for its difficulty and intricate world-building, the Dark Souls trilogy is a modern classic.

Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice

A samurai-inspired action-adventure game with precise combat mechanics and a stunning world.

Elden Ring

An expansive open-world action RPG that combines the best elements of Soulsborne games with an epic narrative.

Survival Horror

Survival horror games create tense atmospheres, blending action with suspense. Here are my favorites:

Resident Evil Series

Horror-action games blending tense survival gameplay with gripping stories. My favorities would have to be OG RE4, RE2 Remake, and RE7

Dead Space

A sci-fi survival horror game with a haunting atmosphere and gripping gameplay.

Silent Hill

A horror classic known for its psychological storytelling and eerie atmosphere.

Role-Playing Games (RPGs)

RPGs immerse players in expansive worlds with deep narratives and character progression. Here are my picks:

Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic

An epic RPG set in the Star Wars universe, featuring deep lore and choices. Star wars is probably my favorite media franchise ever and this is my favorite game of all time.

Mass Effect Trilogy

An RPG where your decisions shape the fate of the galaxy. Has so many intriguing characters to get attached to.

Dragon Age Trilogy

A fantasy RPG series with rich characters and branching storylines. Another franchise with fascinating characters and companions to connect with.

Puzzle and Simulation

These games are relaxing and engaging, offering unique gameplay experiences:

Stardew Valley

A farming simulation game with charming visuals and endless creativity.

Portal

A clever puzzle game that challenges your mind with unique mechanics.

Fun Facts About Some of These Games

  • Mass Effect was initially planned as an Xbox exclusive before being ported to other platforms.
  • Dark Souls coined the term "You Died" and inspired an entire genre of challenging games.
  • The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim has been ported to over 14 different platforms since its release in 2011.
  • Red Dead Redemption 2 took over 8 years to develop and has one of the largest open worlds ever created.
  • Portal was originally a student project before becoming a critically acclaimed game by Valve.
